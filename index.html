Email 18: No Case Study? Best Offer Is To Guarantee Results!

Note: Seriously, if you’re going to guarantee results like this you need to be willing to put your money where your mouth is. If you don’t get them results then you will be required to actually refund them. The power of this is it makes it EASY to get the sale, but it also means you need to GET results for them.

The con is: it can take extra work, risk, and be stressful actually trying to help your clients...

The pro is: you can get clients much easier and master the delivery of the service with live clients…

Best for if you start off delivering the services yourself, that way there are no costs to failing beyond losing time, at least you learn something. You also need to vet your prospects, to make sure what they have will actually sell well, meaning it’s already selling elsewhere and you can find their clients on the platform you’re using e.g Facebook ads.

Subject: About Company & [your company name]

Body:

Hi {{FIRST_NAME}},

As the {{TITLE}} at {{COMPANY}} I had to try to get in contact as we help {{INDUSTRY}} companies like yours [Get Big Result].

We can help {{COMPANY}} achieve [big result] through [your service] in a ‘done-for-you’ manner. [Note: you can change this sentence entirely to anything you like as long as you briefly tell them something you can do for them that they want]

In fact we literally guarantee that you will get results, or we work for free, it’s our policy.

Do you have time to talk some time this or next week?
